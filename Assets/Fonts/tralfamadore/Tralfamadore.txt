4 - The atmosphere of Tralfamadore is pure cyanide. 
5 - There are five sexes on Tralfamadore, each of them performing a step necessary in the creation of a new individual.

7 - Tralfamadorians have the ability to look at any moment in time that interests them and ignore the bad times to concentrate on the good ones. 
8 - When Tralfamadorians sees a corpse, all that they think is that the dead person is in a bad condition in that particular moment, but the same person is just fine in plenty of other moments. 9 - Tralfamadorians are two feet high, green and shaped like plumbers' friends, with the suction cups on the ground and the shafts pointing skywards. At the top of the shaft is a little hand with a green eye in its palm. 
10 - Tralfamadorians can see in four dimensions.
